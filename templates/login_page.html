FAQ’s

Here are our most frequently asked questions

What is the Problem Statement ?

In educational institutions, managing assignments and providing timely, accurate feedback is a critical aspect of the learning process. However, traditional methods of assignment submission, evaluation, and grading can be time-consuming and prone to inefficiencies. The challenge is to develop an Assignment Management and Grading System that streamlines the entire process, from assignment submission to grading, while ensuring transparency and efficiency.

Define the problem statement in brief ?

●Inefficient Assignment Submission: Current assignment submission processes may involve manual paperwork, making it cumbersome for students and teachers alike.
● Delayed Feedback: The turnaround time for grading and providing feedback to students can be lengthy, impacting the learning experience.
● Lack of Transparency: Students often lack visibility into the grading process, leading to uncertainties and misunderstandings about their academic performance.

What is the Objective ?

The primary goal of this challenge is to design and implement an Assignment Management and Grading System that automates the assignment submission process, facilitates efficient grading, and ensures transparent communication of feedback to students.

What are the Functional Requirements ?

1. Online Assignment Submission:
○ Create a user-friendly platform for students to submit assignments digitally, including file uploads and relevant details.
2. Automated Grading System:
○ Implement an automated grading system that evaluates assignments based on predefined criteria, providing instant feedback on objective assessments. 3. Feedback Mechanism:
○ Develop a mechanism for teachers to provide qualitative feedback on assignments, explaining the evaluation criteria and offering constructive suggestions for improvement.
4. Real-time Progress Tracking:
○ Enable students to track the progress of their assignments in real-time, from submission to grading, fostering transparency and reducing uncertainty.
5. Plagiarism Detection:
○ Incorporate plagiarism detection tools to ensure the originality of assignments and maintain academic integrity.
6. Gradebook Management:
○ Develop a centralized gradebook that compiles all assignment grades, providing a comprehensive overview for both teachers and students.
